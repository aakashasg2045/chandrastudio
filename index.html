About Our Studio

The Story Behind Chandra's Studio

Founded in November 2003, Chandra Photography Studio has been capturing timeless moments with precision, passion, and a keen artistic eye for over two decades. With a strong commitment to excellence and creativity, Chandra has built a trusted reputation for delivering high-quality photography services across a wide range of genres—including portraits, weddings, commercial, and fine art photography.

At the heart of the studio is a belief in storytelling through imagery. Every photograph is carefully crafted to reflect the personality, emotion, and essence of the subject. With years of experience and a loyal client base, Chandra Photography Studio continues to evolve—embracing new techniques and technologies while maintaining a timeless, authentic approach to visual storytelling.
